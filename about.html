How it started?

This year, I started a quest to walk all the roads of Vancouver and ended up on many adventures along the way (read more on the blog here: SaunterVan).

Once I started, I realised quickly that I needed an 'assistant' to track the roads I've been on and the ones I have yet to explore, and get a sense of my progression walking around the various neighbourhoods in the mainland. That's how Gogomi was born. What started as some hacked together scripts ended up becoming a full fledged project because my friends and met others who also wanted something like this for various reasons (some to explore roads, others to walk more, many different reasons)

Gogomi maps your progress, highlighting unexplored streets like a personal coloring book of your neighborhood. Whether you're a casual walker or urban explorer, it adds purpose and fun to every outing.

- Kevin
