Privacy Policy

Gogomi (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our app (the “Service”). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your username and passcode to authenticate and manage your access to the Service.

2. Workout Data

With your explicit permission, we access GPS route data from your walk workouts recorded via Apple Watch. This data is processed to generate aggregated insights and is not stored as raw data. Processed data is tied to your account.

3. Permissions

We require your consent to access HealthKit and location data from workouts recorded on your Apple Watch. Permissions can be managed and revoked at any time through your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

1. To Provide and Improve the Service

We use your data to process workout information, generate route insights, and maintain your account to deliver personalized features.

2. To Ensure Data Accuracy and Security

Your data is used solely for route aggregation and app functionality.

3. To Communicate with You

We may respond to your support requests via our support email.

Data Retention

Processed workout data and account information are retained as long as your account remains active. If you request to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal or processed data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is used exclusively for the functionality of the Service.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data by employing encryption for data transfer and secure storage mechanisms. While we strive to protect your personal information, no system is completely secure. Please use a strong passcode to safeguard your account.

Your Rights

1. Revoke Permissions

You can revoke permissions to collect or process HealthKit and location data at any time through your device settings.

2. Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us via the support email.
